About Pinkel

What began as a simple question, has turned into an annual gathering.
Below - Founding Fathers of Pinkelfest

What is Pinkelfest?

Pinkelfest is our own little Holiday, created by 3 friends. What began as a simple question, has turned into an annual gathering where friends eat, drink, enjoy cigars, hangout and play games including cornhole, giant jenga, and frisbee barrel toss.
Wiffle ball used to be the most common game the Pinklers would play, but was soon retired from Pinkelfest in 2017.

What is Pinkelwurst?

Pinkelwurst is a traditional German food that is usually served with Kale.
It's key ingredients are: beef fat, pork fat, oats, onions, salts, soy proteins and various spices.

What is served at Pinkelfest?

Besides Pinkelwurst some basic staples include: White Castle, Baked Beans, Onion Rings and Bratwurst.
You can view more foods at the Pinkel Menu.

What is the drink of choice?

Beer and Cactus Juice! Originally, when we had no money, we used to drink as much bad beer as possible.
We have since matured in taste, like a fine wine, and now serve only quality beer, i.e. Spaten, Dinkelacker.
We even include Home Brews including Burch Irish Red and Burch Brown beers.

What is Cactus Juice?

According to most Pinkeler's, Cactus Juice is the libation of the Gods. It is a margarita flavored schnapps that DeKuypers USED to produce.
It is only available in limited quantities. We have cleared out most liquor stores in the Northeast.
We are always on the lookout for more hidden caches. The bottles are easily identifiable by the inch or so of dust that inevitably forms on the top of the bottles. The Pinkel tradition with Cactus Juice, involves drinking it from The Green Tupperware Bowl with lots of ice.
